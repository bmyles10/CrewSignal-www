Beta program agreement

Thanks for being one of the first to try CrewSignal. This page covers what the beta actually includes, what you're responsible for, and confirms we're on the same page before we connect your account.

What the beta includes

  • Completely free. No setup fee, no monthly cost, no credit card required, for as long as you're in the beta.
  • No obligation. You can stop at any time, no contract, no cancellation fee, no hard feelings.
  • Early software. This is a new product. Most of it is tested and working, but as a beta tester you may run into rough edges. If something breaks, tell me and I'll fix it fast, that's the whole point of a beta.
  • In exchange, I'd love your honest feedback. What worked, what didn't, and if it goes well, I'd really appreciate a quick testimonial or a screenshot of a review that came in through CrewSignal.

What CrewSignal does

Once connected, CrewSignal sends one text message to your customer after a job is marked complete or a payment goes through, depending on your setup. The text includes your business name, a short thank-you, and a link to leave a review. Every message includes an opt-out instruction ("Reply STOP").

Hi Jordan, thanks for choosing Rutherford Roofing! We'd love your feedback. Leave us a quick Google review here: g.page/r/rutherford

Reply STOP to opt out

What you're responsible for

Because the text goes out to your customer using your business name, you, not CrewSignal, are the one with the relationship to that customer. That means:

  • You collect your customers' phone numbers as part of doing business with them (booking, invoicing, checkout, however you normally do it today).
  • You let your customers know, at the point of service, that they'll receive a one-time text asking for a review after the job or visit is complete. A simple verbal mention or a line on your invoice/intake form is enough, there's no special script required.
  • You control the timing. CrewSignal only sends once you mark a job complete (or a payment finishes), not on a fixed schedule.

This is the same consent standard already laid out in our Terms & Conditions and SMS Terms. This page is just confirming you've seen it and agree to it before we turn anything on.
