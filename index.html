How it works

Four steps. One of them is yours.

CrewSignal plugs into the software you already use to run jobs. After setup, the only step you do is the one you already do: finish the job.

1

Finish the job

You mark the job complete in your CRM or booking app, exactly like you do today.

2

CrewSignal detects it

Your software notifies CrewSignal automatically the instant the job closes.

3

Customer gets a text

A branded, friendly review request goes out, with your Google link and an opt-out line.

4

Reviews roll in

Happy customers tap the link and leave a review. Your rating climbs on its own.

You're in control

We won't text a customer you didn't mean to text.

Every contractor we've talked to asks the same thing: "What if the job went sideways and I don't want a robot asking that customer for five stars?" Fair. Here's how it actually works.

How the timing works

You decide when "complete" means complete

  • CrewSignal only sends a text when YOU mark the job complete in your CRM, not before
  • If a job went bad, don't mark it complete until you're ready. The review request follows your lead, not a clock
  • No surprise texts firing off because of a status change you didn't make
What we're building next

Even more control, on the way

  • Your message, your wording. Every text sounds like it came from you

FAQ

Questions, answered plainly.

Do I have to change how I work?

No. You keep using the same software to run and close jobs. CrewSignal listens for the "job complete" signal from your CRM and handles the review request automatically. The only step you do is the one you already do.

Which software do you work with?

CrewSignal works with Jobber and Square Appointments today. If you use something else like Housecall Pro, JobNimbus, Spectora, HoneyBook, or anything else, get in touch. We add integrations based on what real customers actually use.

Is text-message review collection allowed?

Yes, when done correctly. CrewSignal uses a carrier-registered sending number, includes a clear opt-out ("Reply STOP") in every message, and honors every opt-out permanently. You're responsible for letting your customers know at the time you collect their number that they'll get a review request text, which we cover in our Terms and SMS Terms.

What does the text actually say?

A short, friendly message with the customer's name, your business name, your Google review link, and an opt-out line. You can customize the wording so it sounds like you, not a corporate notification.

Will my repeat customers get spammed every visit?

No. If someone comes in every week for a fade or a lineup, they won't get hit with a review request every single time. That's exactly the kind of thing that makes a business look spammy, and we hear that concern a lot. CrewSignal sends one review request per completed job, and duplicate signals are automatically detected and ignored.

What does it cost to get started?

It depends on your business size and job volume, so we'll put together pricing that actually fits your situation rather than a flat number that doesn't. We're currently accepting a limited number of Founding Member spots with a rate that's locked in for life once you join. Setup covers connecting your software, configuring your sending number, and getting your message dialed in. Request a demo below and we'll walk you through pricing.

How fast can I be up and running?

Most setups take a few days, mostly waiting on carrier registration for your sending number. Once that clears, reviews start flowing after your very next completed job.

What if I don't have a Google Business Profile yet?

No problem, a lot of contractors don't. We'll walk you through setting one up in about 5 minutes as part of onboarding. See the setup guide here.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract. Cancel anytime; details are in our Terms.
