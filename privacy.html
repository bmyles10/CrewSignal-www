Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Brandon Myles, doing business as CrewSignal ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our website and SMS automation services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you request a demo, fill out a form, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and business details. For our core service operations, we also process end-customer phone numbers and names provided by our business clients solely for the purpose of sending transaction-based review requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, communicate with you, and ensure compliance with our Terms & Conditions. End-customer data is used strictly to execute the automated SMS review requests requested by our business clients.

3. SMS Data Sharing (Strictly Enforced)

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

4. Opt-Out & Contact

You may opt out of receiving text messages from us at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our main website form.
